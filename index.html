Institution: FGV - Getulio Vargas Foundation

Modality: On-site

Start Date: March/2017

Duration: 13 months

Total Hours: 432 classroom hours

Program Overview: The LL.M in Business Law program aims to specialize professionals by enhancing technical, theoretical, and behavioral skills to add value to graduates in the job market. Throughout the program, participants will develop competencies including:

  • Proficiency in corporate law practice
  • Analysis and application of theoretical foundations related to the use of law in business reality
  • Utilization of knowledge from other areas to analyze legal issues

Program Modules:

  1. Compliance: Covers the history of compliance, anti-corruption legislation, compliance management systems, and case studies.
  2. Contracts and Business Practices: Focuses on contracts in various business sectors, financial instruments, and contract guarantees.
  3. Consumer Law and Civil Liability: Explores consumer protection laws, liability for defective products/services, and traditional and contemporary damage repair methods.
  4. Business Labor Law: Covers individual labor contracts, working hours, termination, and judicial resolution of labor disputes.
  5. Regulatory Law: Discusses government intervention in the economy, regulatory agencies, and regulatory control mechanisms.
  6. Corporate Law - Ltda.: Focuses on limited liability companies, shareholder rights and obligations, dissolution procedures, and corporate governance.
  7. Strategies for Dispute Resolution: Includes mediation, arbitration, and litigation strategies for resolving business conflicts.
  8. Fundamentals of Accounting and Tax Law: Covers accounting principles and basic tax law concepts relevant to business operations.
  9. Strategic Management of Legal Services: Discusses legal administration, strategic planning, administrative routines, and pricing.
  10. Bidding and Administrative Contracts: Covers bidding processes, public-private partnerships, and management of administrative contracts.
  11. Legal Marketing: Explores multidisciplinary competencies essential for legal business management and marketing strategies.
  12. Data Protection: Covers data protection laws, compliance frameworks, and privacy governance.
  13. Business Recovery: Focuses on business insolvency laws, bankruptcy procedures, and corporate restructuring.
  14. Public Companies: Covers the organization and management of public companies, shareholder rights, and corporate reorganization.
  15. Negotiation Techniques for Lawyers: Discusses negotiation strategies and their application in legal contexts.
  16. Taxation of Business Operations: Covers various taxes applicable to businesses, including VAT, corporate income tax, and social contributions.

This comprehensive LL.M program equips graduates with specialized knowledge and practical skills to excel in the dynamic field of business law, preparing them for leadership roles in legal practice, corporate governance, and regulatory compliance.
